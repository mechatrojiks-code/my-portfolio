Who I Am

I'm a BS Mechatronics Engineering graduate from Batangas State University, currently working as a Test Engineer at Kinpo Electronics Philippines. My role sits at the intersection of hardware, software, and data. I work with real production test data every day in a manufacturing environment.


I understand how data is physically collected, what causes it to break, and how to interpret it in context. I have written SQL to pull test results, maintain Python scripts for testing automation, and trained ML models on real sensor datasets.
