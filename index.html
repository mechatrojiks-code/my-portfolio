ML-Based Physio-Behavioral Analysis with Data Logging
High chick mortality rates are often caused by undetected early stress or illness. Built an ML system that monitors physio-behavioral patterns using multiple environment sensors — including a thermal camera to track body temperature, alongside other sensors measuring surrounding conditions. All readings are automatically logged and structured in Excel, giving farmers a clear data record to detect at-risk chicks early and act before losses occur.
